Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad - کهگیلویه و بویراحمد

English

Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad Province (Persian: استان کهگیلویه و بویراحمد, Ostān-e Kohgīlūye va Būyer-Ahmad ) (Luri: استان کهگیلویه و بِیرَمَد, Ostān-e Kohgīlūya-vo Beyramad ) is one of the thirty-one provinces of Iran. It is in the south-west of the country, in Iran's Region 2,[3] and its capital is Yasuj. The province covers an area of 15,563 square kilometers, and in 2006 had a population of 634,000. According to the National Population and Housing Census, the population of Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad in 2011 was 658,629. The people mainly speak the Luri language. The counties of Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad Province are Bahmai County, Landeh County, Boyer-Ahmad County, Charam County, Dana County, Basht County and Gachsaran County.

Deutsch

Kohgiluye und Boyer Ahmad (persisch کهگیلویه و بویراحمد Kohgīlūya va Boir-Aḥmad) ist eine der 31 Provinzen im Iran. Hauptstadt ist Yasudsch. In der Provinz leben 713.052 Menschen (Volkszählung 2016). Die Fläche der Provinz erstreckt sich auf 15.504 Quadratkilometer. Die Bevölkerungsdichte beträgt 46 Einwohner pro Quadratkilometer.
